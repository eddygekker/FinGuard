# FinGuard Retention Playbook

Expert interventions for B2B SaaS churn prevention.

## High risk (score 65+) — act within 48 hours
- Executive-aligned CSM call (30 min) referencing specific usage decline and open issues
- Escalate every open ticket to senior support with 24h SLA; CSM follows up same day
- If payment failures: billing call + one-time 10–15% renewal credit or payment plan
- If feature adoption < 2: schedule live re-onboarding on top 2 unused core features
- Document save plan in CRM with owner, deadline, and success criteria

## Medium risk (score 35–64) — act within 5 days
- Personalized email from named CSM (not automation) citing last login date
- Share 1 industry-specific use case + 5-min Loom for underused features
- Review support history; proactively close loop on past friction
- Set 14-day usage checkpoint; trigger alert if no improvement

## Low risk (score 0–34)
- Standard QBR cadence for Enterprise; monthly pulse for Pro/Basic
- Invite to webinar or case study in their industry

## Signal-specific playbooks
- **Usage drop > 40%:** Root-cause call — product friction vs. champion left vs. competitor
- **No login 7+ days:** Re-engagement sequence day 1 email, day 2 call, day 4 exec offer
- **Open tickets 2+:** War-room with support lead; CSM owns customer communication
- **Payment failures:** Same-day outreach; never wait for next billing cycle
- **Low adoption on Pro/Enterprise:** Mandatory success session before renewal window
